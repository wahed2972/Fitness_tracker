Workout 5

Description

  • Starting Position:
    • Sit on a sturdy, upright bench or chair with back support.
    • Hold a dumbbell in each hand at shoulder height with palms facing forward.
    • Feet flat on the floor, shoulder-width apart.
  • Engage Your Core:
    • Tighten your core muscles for stability.
  • Press the Weights:
    • Exhale as you press the dumbbells upward.
    • Extend your arms fully overhead, palms facing forward.
  • Hold at the Top:
    • At the top, arms fully extended but not locked.
    • Squeeze your shoulder muscles briefly.
  • Lower the Weights:
    • Inhale as you lower the dumbbells back to shoulder height with control.
  • Repeat:
    • Perform the desired number of repetitions. Sets of 8-12 reps are common.

Sets:3

Rep:10

Additional Details

  • Use proper form: Maintain a neutral spine throughout the exercise, and avoid arching your back.
  • Start with an appropriate weight: Choose a weight that allows you to perform the exercise with good form and control.
  • Control the tempo: Don't rush the movement; focus on controlled lifting and lowering of the dumbbells.
  • Warm up your shoulders: Prior to the shoulder press, warm up your shoulder muscles with dynamic stretches or light shoulder exercises.
  • Consult a fitness professional: If you're new to strength training or have any shoulder issues, consider seeking guidance from a fitness expert to ensure you're performing the exercise safely.
